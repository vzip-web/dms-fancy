About DMS Fancy Shop

Sri Lanka's premier destination for luxury beauty – trusted, authentic, premium.

Our Story

Born from a Passion for Beauty

DMS Fancy Shop was founded with a single vision: to bring the world's most premium beauty products directly to the doorsteps of Sri Lankans — at fair prices with outstanding service.

Located in the heart of Negombo at 71 S.W.R Road, 4th Kurana, we have served hundreds of satisfied customers across the island. From Colombo to Kandy, Galle to Jaffna — we deliver beauty, radiance, and confidence islandwide.

Our collection is carefully curated: only authentic, tested, and high-performance products make it to our shelf. We believe every Sri Lankan deserves access to luxury skincare, glutathione formulas, and premium haircare.
